Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

From tales of tradition, from dusty workshops and the smell of the earth, Molela Terracotta was born. It was shaped by the potter, on his wheel and by the carpenter’s steady hand. By the prick of the needle and the chisel on stone. This is the world where our stories are told. In places and practices from time immemorial, in folklore and fables that have somehow been forgotten. Handcrafted to glorious imperfection, each piece in the Molela Terracotta collection recalls the story of it’s own identity. Akkaara brings you premium lifestyle products inspired by folklore and traditional crafts. Our products are handcrafted by artisans who have honed their skills over generations of practice. Each product in the Molela Terracotta collection is designed with intricate care and concern. Concern for tradition, concern for the material and concern for the artisans. Through each product, Molela Terracotta strives to revive what has been lost – the exquisite splendour of traditional arts, the cultural heritage of generations of craftspeople and the memory of age-old stories, embedded in traditional wisdom and folklore.
